NASA Team Task Switching

I currently work as an undergraduate research assistant at the ATLAS Lab at Northwestern. My most recent project with ATLAS has been conducted in cooperation with NASA to study astronauts being held in isolation in Russia's NEK. The purpose of this study is to understand differences in team dynamic and leadership that may arise while in space.
